# Design Intent — TeamPulse

> **Declaration-truth for look, feel, and experience.** Owned by **skill 00**. Skill 02 **realizes** this into a
> design system + screens that *reference* it, changing intent only via amendment. `derived` items are skill-00
> inferences (the PRD says little about look/feel) — confirm before treating as fixed.

## Brand & feeling

- **Adjectives (3–5):** calm, focused, low-interruption — a quiet utility, not a social feed. _(derived)_
- **Tone / voice:** plain and unobtrusive; the digest is skimmed, not read. _(derived)_
- **References / anti-references:** NOT a chat/social surface; closer to a clean daily briefing. _(derived)_

## Key moments

- **The digest is the home.** One screen, scannable top-to-bottom in under two minutes for a 12-person team, with
  "needs help" blockers first. _(stated — success criteria; REQ-009 / REQ-010)_
- **Zero-training team setup.** A lead creates, invites, and configures a team with no manual. _(stated — "with no
  training")_
- **Async submission.** A member answers three prompts on their own schedule, no one online at once. _(derived — async
  premise; REQ-001)_
