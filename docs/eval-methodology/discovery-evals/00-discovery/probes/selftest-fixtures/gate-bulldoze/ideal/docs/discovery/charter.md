## Job to be Done

> The canonical job the product is hired for. Every requirement in the spine traces back to this.

**When** I keep houseplants and keep forgetting to water them, **I want** a reliable per-plant reminder, **so I
can** stop losing plants to neglect.

## Problem & user

- **Problem:** Houseplant owners lose plants not from ignorance but from forgetting — a schedule that lives in the
  owner's head fails silently.
- **Target user:** A single person managing their own plant list on one device.
- **How they solve it today / what's painful:** Mental tracking or a generic calendar reminder that doesn't
  account for different plants needing different intervals.

## Scope

- **In scope (v1, draft):** Add a plant, set a per-plant watering interval, local reminder notifications, mark
  watered, snooze a single reminder.
- **Out of scope (v1, explicitly excluded):** Shared households, plant-care social feed, plant-ID camera,
  marketplace. Named here to prevent scope creep — these are not registry rows.
